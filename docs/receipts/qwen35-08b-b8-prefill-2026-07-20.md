## Correctness

`smelt lab verify` compared the candidate against the detached baseline over
the four default prompts for four generated tokens. Every token sequence was
identical. Prefill-vs-decode parity passed at lengths 1, 4, 7, 8, 9, and 16.

The focused Metal test compares both `_b8` functions against the staged B16
matvec-plus-residual path, including residual/output aliasing. Intermediate
matvec and final FP16 outputs had zero bit difference. The deterministic source
QMM sweep also requires exact output equality before reporting timings.

## Kernel evidence

The source sweep used batch 8, 10 measured iterations, and 2 warmups:

| fused projection | B16 median | B8 median | delta |
| --- | ---: | ---: | ---: |
| c2048 → r1024 | 185.42 µs | 163.37 µs | -11.9% |
| c3584 → r1024 | 314.96 µs | 275.12 µs | -12.6% |

A reverse-order 50-iteration package profile isolated the same two families:
c2048 fell from 4,480 to 3,912 µs per prefill (-12.7%), c3584 fell from 7,599
to 6,610 µs (-13.0%), and profiled total GPU time fell from 28,143 to 26,638
µs (-5.3%). Other named kernel families were effectively unchanged. Full-name
profile output confirms the candidate executed the `_b8` functions.
